Vapor-Liquid Equilibrium

Vapor-liquid equilibrium conditions are calculated for mixtures of up to six components. Compositions of either the liquid or vapor phase can be specified, from which the equilibrium phase conditions can be calculated. Flash calculations for incoming streams can also be solved.

- Requires downloaded 'Components' database -

Features:

  • Component data in database shared/accessible to all thermodynamic applications
  • Solve equilibrium for known conditions of either liquid or vapor output streams
  • Solve equilibrium for known temperature or pressure conditions
  • Checks for validity of components below critical temperatures
  • Solve equilibrium for flash calculations including quality
  • Information on the development of the vapor-liquid equilibrium algorithm is presented
  • Instructions for the software application is presented

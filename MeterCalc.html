Flow Meter Calculations

Venturi and orifice meters are designed for size, flow rate and pressure drop. Calculations for velocity through the meter, Reynolds number, discharge coefficient and expansion factor are shown. Fluids include both non-compressible and compressible fluids.

Features:

  • Venturi or orifice meters can be modeled for flow measurement
  • Pipe Tap configurations can be specified for more accurate real-world measurements
  • Standard USS pipe sizes can be selected from options
  • Pipe and fluid properties can be specified with a variety of different units
  • Flow can be specified as either mass or volumetric flow
  • Fluid flow or delta P can be calculated for known meter configuration
  • Meter size can be calculated for given flow and pressure drop
  • Instructions for the software application is presented
